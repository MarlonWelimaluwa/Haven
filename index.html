My Story

I was where
you are now.

At 34, I was a Director at a Fortune 500 company — respected, promoted, and completely falling apart behind closed doors. I cried in my car before every Monday morning meeting. I called it stress. My doctor called it burnout. I called it a wake-up call.

After two years of therapy, coach training, and learning the hard way, I rebuilt my life on my own terms. I left corporate, became an ICF-certified burnout recovery coach, and have since helped over 300 high-achieving professionals do what I did — without losing everything in the process.

I don't do toxic positivity. I don't do generic life advice. I do deep, honest, practical work — tailored entirely to you.

FAQ

You probably have questions.

That's completely natural. Here are the ones I hear most often. Still unsure? The discovery call has zero pressure — just ask me there.

Book Free Discovery Call
Is this therapy or coaching — what's the difference? +

Coaching is forward-focused — we work on where you want to go and what's getting in the way right now. Therapy tends to focus on processing the past. Both are valuable. If you're experiencing clinical depression or trauma, I'll refer you to a therapist and can work alongside that process.

I'm not sure I'm "burnt out enough" for this. +

If you're running on empty, feeling disconnected, or just quietly dreading your days — that's enough. You don't need to be at rock bottom to ask for support. The earlier we address it, the faster and more lasting the recovery.

How are the sessions delivered? +

All sessions are online via Zoom or Google Meet — from your home, your car, wherever you feel safe and comfortable. I work with clients across the UK, US, Australia, and beyond.

What if I need to pause or cancel? +

Life happens. Sessions can be rescheduled with 24 hours' notice. If circumstances change significantly, we'll find a solution together. I'm not in the business of making hard times harder.

Is everything I share completely confidential? +

Absolutely. Everything shared in sessions is strictly confidential under ICF ethical guidelines. The only exceptions are the standard legal requirements around risk of harm — which I'll explain transparently in our first session.

How do I know if this is right for me? +

That's exactly what the free discovery call is for. 45 minutes, no commitment, no pressure. You get a feel for how I work, I understand where you are, and we're both honest about whether it's a good fit. Most people leave feeling clearer just from that conversation.
